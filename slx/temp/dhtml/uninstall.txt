How to uninstall Ferant DHTML Studio Lite.

Since there is no installation has been made on you computer, you need only remove files you have unzipped from FerantStudioLite.zip. Let say, you chose c:/Ferant as you unzip folder. Here are file to remove (none of them are executable)

1. c:/Ferant/readme.txt, c:/Ferant/uninstall.txt, c:/Ferant/index.htm, c:/Ferant/_ns.htm, c:/Ferant/License.txt

2. c:/Ferant/inc/edit/js, c:/Ferant/inc/FerantLib/js

3. All image files in c:/Ferant/img
